Logo

Brand mark, clear space, and usage guidelines

Primary Mark

The primary logo mark consists of the wordmark rendered in Inter Semibold with tight letter-spacing. The mark should always be reproduced from the provided SVG assets — never redrawn, recolored, or distorted.

Clear Space

Maintain a minimum clear space equal to the height of the capital "P" around all sides of the logo. No other elements should intrude into this area.

Usage Guidelines

✓ Do — Light background

Place the logo on the brand background colour (#f5f5f3) or white. Use the full-colour mark.

✗ Don't — Recolour

Never apply gradients, drop shadows, or additional colours to the logo mark.

✗ Don't — Distort

Never stretch, skew, rotate, or disproportionately scale the logo.

✗ Don't — Rearrange

Never rearrange the elements, change the typeface, or alter the letter-spacing.
